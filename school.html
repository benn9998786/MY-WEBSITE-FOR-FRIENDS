Fr. Agnel Higher Secondary School

H I S T O R Y

Fr. Agnel Higher Secondary School was started with Arts and Commerce streams by Fr. Philomeno D'Costa, S.F.X in June 1979.

In June 1982, the Science Stream was started. Later, vocational training was introduced.

Fr. Agnel School is run under the Xaverian Educational Society.

Ven. Fr. Agnel D'souza

Peace-loving priest born on 21st Jan1869 in Anjuna, Goa

Name meaning: “Little lamb.”
